About — What we do

Established in 2017 under SA Hobby Council, UB Photo Club is a family of photographers from various levels of experience. Together, we participate in weekly meetings and special events to cultivate our curiosity for life through photography. Our club core values are to learn, connect, and share.

Learn

Attend our weekly meetings to learn more about photography gear, photographing and editing techniques, and analyzing photography in pop-culture.

Connect

Take the time to check in to our special events to enjoy unique activities and take the opportunity to establish connections with our amazing community.

Share

Join our Facebook Group and Messenger Group to share what you have, take advantage of what other people have to share, and participate in votes.
